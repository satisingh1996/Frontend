Hotel Information


This is our first step for helping the needy persons . And we are tie up with a lot of hotels who helps the people in a worst situation. It is a hotel steeped in societal consciousness and partakes in a lot of charity work. As a result, it has inspired a lot of other hotels to take up similar activities. The hotel allows guests to take a leading role when it comes to serving the poor. For example, whenever guests reach in the hotel, they are actually helping fund ‘Feed’, an initiative that supplies food and micronutrients to malnourished children all over the globe.
